Bags'R'Us

In today's world where recycling is a priority and plastic bags become a limited and payed for commodity, we aim to provide companies in need of bags for their customers quality, colourful, branded plastic bags.

Our bags are produced responsibly, with minimum pollution. They are recyclable and made from recyclable materials.

We are situated in the fabulous location of the University of Stirling.
