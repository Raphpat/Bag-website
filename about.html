About Bags'R'Us

We are a small invented company that aims to supply bags to any shop that needs bags for its customers.
Our bags are customisable and sturdy, all that is needed to strive in today's world.
Furthermore, all materials are of recycled origin.

The Assignement

This website was created for the CSCU9B2 Assignement, in the second semester of the 2018/2019 academic year. Students had to create a website to "sell" carrier bags. A canvas should be used to draw a bag with text on it. There should be a choice of colours for both the bag and the text. The "order" button should create a popup that informs the user that the order has been placed.
